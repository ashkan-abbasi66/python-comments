Penn-Fudan Database for Pedestrian Detection and Segmentation

Copy data into "PennFudanPed".

Here is the dataset:
	https://www.cis.upenn.edu/~jshi/ped_html/

There are 170 images with 345 labeled pedestrians, among which 96 images are taken from around 
University of Pennsylvania, and other 74 are taken from around Fudan University.
